Our Story

More Than a Meal

Lookin Good Restaurant and Lounge has been a beloved Denver institution — a place where neighbors become regulars and strangers become friends over plates of comfort food done right.

From our famous Mad Greek Waffles to golden Saganaki, Country Chicken Fried Steak to hearty Steak and Eggs — every dish is made with love and served with a smile.
